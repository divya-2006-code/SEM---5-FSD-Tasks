🌙 Moon Observation Journal

The moon is one of the most fascinating objects in the night sky. Its appearance changes throughout the month as it moves through different phases. Observing the moon helps us understand the beauty of our universe and the natural cycles of Earth.

Astronomers and sky enthusiasts often record moon phases to track changes in visibility and shape. This journal presents observations made during a lunar cycle and highlights the different stages of the moon's journey across the night sky.
